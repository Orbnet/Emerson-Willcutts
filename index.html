Artistic Style

My art takes the grittier, darker elements of life and transforms it into something more lighthearted and comedic. I do this to remind those who have forgotten either truth of life, whether it be the messy or neat parts, of what it is to be human.

Fiction

The category of writing that I excel the most at would be fiction. I have had a nack for it ever since I've started writing, and if you're curious what it is like just look at this piece, The Broken Message

Graphic Design

My graphic design particularlly revolves around programs such as website layouts and designs, along with modifying images to fit their setting.

Poetry

The approach I take towards poetry is an exact opposite of how I treat fiction. It is a more serious take on the same inconsistencies of the human condition.
